Signature From Start to Finish

Model S and Model X marked the beginning of the world's transition to electric transportation. Our limited-release Signature Edition honors their legacy—and made way for what's next.

Model X is no longer in production. Pre-owned non-Signature Edition inventory vehicles may still be available.

Next-Level Interior

Three Ultra-Responsive Displays

A 17.4" first-row and 9.4" second-row touchscreen offer a vivid image and respond quickly to your touch. Driver cluster display shows your speed, range and more.

Relax in the Back

Spacious second and third rows seats five adults comfortably. Wireless phone charging keeps devices powered.

Get Comfortable

Integrated air vents, tri-zone climate controls and HEPA filtration create your ideal cabin atmosphere.

Entertainment for Everyone

Play your favorite games, movies or songs from first- and second-row touchscreens. Stream wherever you travel with Premium Connectivity, no Wi-Fi needed.²

Full-Spectrum Color

Choose from infinite colors to customize your wrap-around lighting and enjoy dynamic animated illuminations that appear along the dash and door trims when you enter then fade to an ambient glow.

Maximum Comfort

Supportive, ventilated front-row seats and heated seats throughout the cabin provide added comfort for every passenger.

Always Connected

Multi-device Bluetooth provides wireless connectivity and convenience for all passengers.

Studio-Quality Sound

Features 22 speakers, a 960-watt audio system and sound insulation that gives you clear, recording studio-grade sound quality.

The Future of Travel Is Autonomous

Enter your destination and your Model X will manage your drive, from start to finish. Full Self-Driving (Supervised) provides advanced features that offer more active guidance and assisted driving under your active supervision.
