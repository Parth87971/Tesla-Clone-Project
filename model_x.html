Signature From Start to Finish

Model S and Model X marked the beginning of the world's transition to electric transportation. Our limited-release Signature Edition honors their legacy—and made way for what's next.

Model X is no longer in production. Pre-owned non-Signature Edition inventory vehicles may still be available.

Next-Level Interior

Three Ultra-Responsive Displays

A 17.4" first-row and 9.4" second-row touchscreen offer a vivid image and respond quickly to your touch. Driver cluster display shows your speed, range and more.

Full-Spectrum Color

Choose from infinite colors to customize your wrap-around lighting and enjoy dynamic illuminations that appear along the dash and door trims when you enter then fade to an ambient glow.

Maximum Comfort

Supportive, ventilated front-row seats and heated seats throughout the cabin provide added comfort for every passenger. Bluetooth keeps you connected.

The Future of Travel Is Autonomous

Enter your destination and your Model X will manage your drive, from start to finish. Full Self-Driving (Supervised) provides advanced features that offer more active guidance.
