Charge at Home

Charge your Model X at home to wake up charged and start your day with plenty of range, no gas station needed—ever. Learn more about home charging.

Home Charging Options

  • ⚡
    Tesla Wall Connector — Delivers up to 44 miles of range per hour. Install indoors or outdoors. Full charge in ~8 hours.
  • ⚡
    NEMA 14-50 Outlet — Standard 240V outlet delivers ~30 miles of range per hour. Full charge in ~10 hours.
  • ⚡
    Mobile Connector — Included with every Tesla. Plugs into standard household outlets for convenient overnight charging.
  • ⚡
    Scheduled Charging — Set your car to start charging at off-peak hours to save on electricity costs.

Safety Comes Standard

Safety at Tesla is paramount. Every Tesla vehicle is designed with impact-absorbing zones, a stiff passenger compartment to minimize intrusion and multiple airbags to help protect occupants.

Active safety features can help reduce impact severity or help prevent accidents altogether. Forward Collision Warning, Active Emergency Braking and Lane Departure Avoidance come standard.

The passive and active safety systems are engineered to make Tesla vehicles the safest in the world with a very low probability of injury.
