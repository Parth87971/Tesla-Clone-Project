Building a Sustainable, More Resilient Grid

The future of renewable energy relies on large-scale industrial energy storage. Megapack is a powerful, integrated battery system that provides clean, reliable, cost-effective energy storage to help stabilize the grid and prevent outages. Reducing our reliance on fossil fuels and strengthening our grid infrastructure will make sustainable energy more accessible and affordable for everyone on Earth. Learn more in our Master Plan Part 3.

Transforming Industrial Energy Storage

Megapack is changing the way the grid is powered. Operating in over 65 countries, Megapack provides critical grid support globally and can support multi-GWh projects.

Easy to Install

Each unit ships fully assembled with a built-in foundation and integrated wireways, reducing installation costs by 25%.² Its flexible configuration allows for faster installation, and over 99% of deployments are delivered on time. We also offer support during installation and throughout operation.

Safe to Operate

Megapack offers reliable and safe energy storage. It is designed as a single, vertically integrated system with hardware and controls that reduce fire risk. It meets over 40 global safety requirements, ensuring a safe environment for site workers and local communities.

Built for Performance

Megapack comes with a 20-year warranty and performance guarantees, ensuring operational capacity throughout the lifetime of the system. Each installation receives 24/7 support and automated diagnostics, with an average uptime of over 99%, even in harsh environments.

Power More

Megapack installations are fully customizable and can be deployed at scale, making them suitable for a variety of project sizes, locations and applications.

Standalone Utilities

Stabilizes grid voltage, frequency and capacity levels, replacing fossil-fuel peaker plants and keeping the grid running smoothly.

Renewable Paired

Maintains the stability and reliability of renewable energy sources on the grid by storing and discharging energy depending on the availability of solar and wind energy.

Data Centers

Provides reliable power to data centers, ensuring continuous power, preventing costly downtime and data loss due to outages.

Commercial and Industrial

Offers commercial businesses more reliable energy access and allows them to share excess energy with the grid during peak demand hours.

Microgrids

Allows electricity to be pooled and then distributed or managed without a connection to the grid.

We Build Big Batteries

Our Megafactories in Lathrop, California and Shanghai, China are among the largest industrial battery manufacturing facilities in the world. Together, they have a total manufacturing capacity of 80 GWh, or 20,000 units, per year. That's enough energy storage to retire 400 fossil fuel peaker plants (100 MW each) every year.

Fully Integrated Software

From design to commissioning and long-term management, Megapack is equipped with software that maximizes system storage performance and minimizes risk while increasing revenue potential over time. Our software is vertically integrated and can control, monitor and optimize the entire Megapack system. This software constantly improves through over-the-air updates and hardware-in-loop testing.

Streamlined Service, Maximum Performance

Tesla provides performance guarantees and lifetime support for all Megapack systems, with the option to let Tesla service your system or your own team, certified by us.

Serviced by Tesla

Tesla handles all service tasks for you, including corrective and preventative maintenance. Our service technicians continuously monitor, diagnose and resolve system issues remotely or on-site to ensure maximum performance.

Certified by Tesla

If you choose not to use Tesla Servicing, Tesla can certify your in-house or third-party team to service your system, giving them access to the same documentation and tools used by Tesla technicians.

Megapack Delivers

Megapack is making clean industrial energy storage more accessible and affordable. As the world adopts more renewable energy sources like solar and wind, batteries help balance supply and demand on the grid. This offers greater stability, preventing grid outages and reducing reliance on fossil fuel peaker plants. Each Megapack 2 XL avoids 88,000 tons of CO₂e.
